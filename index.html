The Story

Souls of the Deep is a riveting story about the life altering adventures of a fish named Billy Moon and his beloved mother, Belle. On Christmas Day while peacefully swimming nearby their home, a gigantic fishing net descends from high above and traps Belle in its lethal clutches. Billy is left frozen and devastated in his tracks as he watches his mother being dragged away before his eyes. Belle struggles to escape the wretched net where she is entangled with hundreds of other frantic fish gasping for their last breath of life.Billy is profoundly stricken with loss. He delves deep into depression, ignoring extended family and friends.

One day, he stumbles upon a message, buried deep in the sand beside a canyon. Slowly, he opens it, and to his astonishment, he discovers a clue that his mother may still be alive.Determined to locate his beloved mom, Billy embarks on a journey along with his best friend Lucas to find her.The twists and turns of their compelling adventures will lure you into a world of love, suspense and intrigue that will leave you glued to your seat. You will want to read more of this gripping story and find a plethora of insight into the human spirit. Discovering meaningful connections to your own inner world. We invite you to join this amazing voyage of love, friendship, loss, and hope. It will touch your heart and soul in places you never dreamed existed and leave you yearning for more.
